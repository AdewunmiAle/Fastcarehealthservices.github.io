Our Story

Nigeria’s health system has struggled to meet the needs of an ever-growing population despite economic advances. When compared globally, Nigeria is ranked 142nd out of 195 countries when evaluating its performance on a comparative healthcare access and quality index. Further, Nigeria frequently loses its trained healthcare providers to other countries, suffering from one of the highest brain drains globally. Nigeria’s ability to deliver accessible quality medical care is limited by its current healthcare infrastructure. Health facilities are poorly resourced and understaffed, especially in rural areas.

Our Vision

Make a user friendly platform and technology accessible to general public. Foster positive relationships between patients and medical professionals.

Our Mission

Active involvement of public in decisions and programs concerning their health. Utilizing technologies to deliver quick medical care to lower premature death.

Our Philosophy

Your health is a top priority. We provide the best possible care to our patients. If you have any question don't hestitate to reach out to us.

Know us well

Our Values

FASTCARE Health Services' goal is to break even in the health sector by offering a technological solution that will lower the nation's premature mortality rate as a result of a poor uptake of medical care. We make it simple for patients to contact a doctor for at-home care, a help van in case of an emergency, and a medical professional to discuss their health condition without going to the hospital.
